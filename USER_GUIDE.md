## 1. Inventory Management
### Adding a New Book
1. Go to the **Inventory** page.
2. Click the green **`+ Add Book`** button.
3. Fill in the **Book ID** (e.g., `B020`), Title, Author, and select the Department.
4. Click **Submit**.

### Deleting a Book
1. Find the book in the Inventory list.
2. Click the red **`Del`** (Trash Icon) button.
3. **Note**: You cannot delete a book if it is currently **Issued**. You must return it first.

## 2. Managing Students & Loans
### Issuing a Book
1. Go to **Inventory** or click **Issue Book** in the Navbar.
2. Enter the **Book ID** (e.g., `B001`) and **Member ID** (e.g., `M001`).
3. Click **Issue Book**.
4. The book status will instantly change to `Issued`.

### Returning a Book
1. Click **Return Book** in the Navbar.
2. Enter the **Book ID**.
3. Click **Return Book**.
4. The system will mark the book as `Available` and log the return date.

### Viewing Student History
1. Go to **Members**.
2. Click on any Student's Name or the **View** button.
3. You will see:
   - **Profile Card**: Name, Dept, Batch.
   - **Active Loans**: Books they have right now.
   - **History**: All past books they have read.

## 3. Dashboard Analytics
- The Home Page shows a Bar Chart of **Department-wise Book Distribution**.
- Refresh the page to see stats update after you add new books!
